About This Hub

Built for Educators, by Educators

Who this is for

This resource hub is designed for faculty, instructional staff, and program leaders who want to teach effectively in an era of rapidly advancing artificial intelligence. Whether you are just beginning to explore AI or already integrating it into your courses, these resources meet you where you are.

How to use it

Start with the Interactive Guides & Tools to build foundational AI literacy and pick up classroom-ready strategies. Then browse the Faculty Repositories to see the specific tools, prompts, and examples your colleagues are using and sharing across the network.
